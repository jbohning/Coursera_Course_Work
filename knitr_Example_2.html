Making Tables with xtable

We want to make a pretty table- not just a standard table

library(datasets)
data(airquality)
fit<-lm(Ozone~Wind+Temp+Solar.R,data=airquality)

Here is a table of regression coefficients.

library(xtable)
xt<-xtable(summary(fit))
print(xt,type="html")
